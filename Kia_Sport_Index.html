Kick Ball 2.0

Instructions:

Divide players into two teams.

Divide one of the teams into 2 lines.

While one the other team throws balls to the lined team, the first person on each line will try to kick the ball.

After kicking the dodgeball you run around the bases with a a baseball glove.

Then the other team tries to hit the runners with dodgeballs

If the throwing team hits a person, not in the glove or head, they are out

After there are 3 outs you switch.

Equipment Needed:

Bases

2 Dodgeballs

Baseball Glove
