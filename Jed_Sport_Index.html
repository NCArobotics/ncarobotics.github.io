Ping-pong Spider web

Instructions:

Divide players into two teams.

Set up two pins on bothe sides of the field you are playing in

Set a middle with a line of dogdeballs

Once you grab a ball you run back to your pins, after this you cannot move if you are holdind a ball - you can only pass it to a team member

to get people out you hit them with your ball, and you get back in if the person who hit you gets out

to win you have to nock down a pin and run it back to your side without getting hit.

Once one team has all the pins, they win!

Equipment Needed:

4 bowling pins

dogdballs
