Sprinkler Volleyball

Instructions:

Divide players into two teams.

The sprinkler should be placed in the center of the two teams.

Determine the boundary of your court. Use cones or string to mark it off.

The object of the game is to get the ball over the sprinkler.

Don't let it touch the ground.

First team to get 15 points, wins the game!

Equipment Needed:

Spinkler

Beach ball or Volleyball

Cones or Rope to mark the court
