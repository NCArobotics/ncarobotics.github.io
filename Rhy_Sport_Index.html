Instructions:

Divide players into two teams.

One team it hopping on 1 foot, while the other team crab-walks

The hopping team gets time to fortify their base(the other team doesn't have enyone invading their base)

The crab-walking team tries to get across to the other team's base, while that team tries to tap them with a foam sword.

If you get tapped you go back to your base, if not, half of the crab- walkers can grab a water weapon(water gun, ballon) and try to shoot the hopping team

If they get hit, they go to 'jail' for 2-3 minutes

The crab-walkers have 15 minutes do invade and defeat the hopping team

Optional: The hopping team can carry weights, and the base can be defended by a sprinkler(can't get wet)


Equipment Needed:

foam swords

water guns and ballons

optional: sprinkler and weights
