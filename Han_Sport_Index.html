Hockey Ping-pong Baseball

Instructions:

Divide players into two teams.

Give out the hockey sticks to one of the teams.

Give the other team the bases and the ping-pong ball.

The team with the bases spread out on a field.

If they can throw and catch the ping-pong ball 5 times in a row, before the other team blocks it with a hockey stick, you get 1 point.

If the team with the hockey sticks can block the ball, they get a point.

Once a point is given, you switch positions.

The first team to 10 points win!


Equipment Needed:

Bases

A Ping-pong ball

Hockey sticks
