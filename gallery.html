Cat Chase

Equipment Needed:

  • Cat
  • Cat Toy
  • String

    • Object:

    Make the other team lose all their 5 points.


    Instructions:


  • Tie a toy onto a string.
    • Run with it.
  • Get the cat to chase the toy.
  • If the cat catches the toy, the cat wins.
  • If the cat doesn't catch the toy, you win.
  • Play it again!

    Sprinkler Volleyball

    Equipment Needed:

  • Spinkler
  • Beach ball or Volleyball
  • Cones or Rope to mark the court

    • Object:

    Get 10 points by getting the ball over the sprinkler and the other team cannot return it.


    Instructions:

  • Divide players into two teams.
  • The sprinkler should be placed in the center of the two teams.
  • Determine the boundary of your court. Use cones or string to mark it off.
  • The object of the game is to get the ball over the sprinkler.
  • Don't let it touch the ground.
  • First team to get 15 points, wins the game!

    Cannonball Puppy

    Equipment Needed:

  • Bathtub
  • 3 tennis balls
  • Timer
  • Dog

    • Object:

    Make the other team lose all their 5 points.


    Instructions:


  • Fill the bathtub with water.
  • Divide players into two teams.
  • Warm your dog up with a quick training session.
  • Start the timer.
  • Throw the first tennis ball down the hallway and have your furry friend fetch!
  • Walk into the bathroom and tell your puppy to "bring it here" and then "drop it" in the tub.
  • Repeat the process with tennis ball #2 and #3.
  • When all 3 tennis balls are in the tub, have your puppy jump in the tub. We use the command "Get in the Tub."
  • When your dog and 3 tennis balls are in the tub, press stop on your timer. Congratulations! This is your new world record time.

    Ping-pong Spider web

    Equipment Needed:

  • Ping-pong Paddles
  • Ping-pong ball
  • Horizantal spider web

    • Object:

    Make the other team lose all their 5 points.


    Instructions:


  • Find a horizantal spider web.
  • Divide players into two teams.
  • Hand out paddles.
  • Each team starts with 5 points.
  • One person starts with a ping-pong ball.
  • Pass it to anyone.
  • when the ball hits the ground: the team that last touched it loses a point.
  • the first team to loose all their points loses.
  • note: this does not have to be played on a web.

    Kick Ball 2.0

    Equipment Needed:

  • 4 Bases
  • 2 Dodgeballs
  • 2 Baseball Gloves

    • Object:

    Get as many runs as you can in the allotted time.


    Instructions:

  • Divide players into two teams.
  • Divide one of the teams into 2 lines.
  • While the other team is throwing balls to the team in the 2 lines, the first person on each line will try to kick the ball.
  • After kicking the dodgeball you run around the bases with a a baseball glove.
  • Then the other team tries to hit the runners with the 2 dodgeballs
  • If the throwing team hits a person, not on the glove or head, they are out
  • After there are 3 outs you switch.

    Make it home!

    Equipment needed:

    Baseball Bat
    Baseballs
    2 Baseball Mitts
    4 Bases


    Object:

    Get 10 points by hitting the ball to home plate.


    Instructions:

  • Set up the field like a baseball field.
  • The batter stands at the pitcher’s mound.
  • The pitcher stands at first base.
  • The batter has 3 tries to hit the ball when the pitcher throws it.
  • A catcher from one team and a catcher from the other team stands by home to try to catch the ball.
  • Players from both teams are in the outfield, trying to catch the ball, pass it, and get it to their catcher.
  • No one can run with the ball.
  • When a catcher touches home with the ball, his or her team gets a point.
  • After two points are scored, they switch.

    Soccerball

    Hahn-Ball

    Equipment Needed:

  • ‘Collaboration Circle’ - we used a mini trampoline
  • Ball
  • 2-4 players

    • Object:

    As a team, bounce the ball off the collaboration circle as many times as you can without any player hitting the ball more than once in a row or allowing the ball to touch the ground.


    Instructions:

  • With two boys, competition can create problems, so we wanted to create a competitive game with a collaborative spin.
  • Work as a team to bounce the ball off the collaboration circle to another player, so they can either pass the ball or they can hit the ball off the collaboration circle to another player.
  • A point is scored each time the ball is passed to another player after bouncing off the ‘Collaboration Circle’.
  • At the beginning of the game, all players discuss their initial goal score. (How many times they think they can pass the ball off the Collaboration Circle to another player before hitting the ground). The key is to start small (5 or less) and then work your way higher. The team must celebrate together each time they achieve or exceed their predetermined goal.
  • Each player can only touch the ball once until it touches another player.
  • If the last person to touch the ball is NOT you, then you can slap, tip or pass the ball to another player or pass it off the Collaboration Circle.
  • If a player touches the ball twice in a row, the scoring stops and you must begin again from 0.
  • If the ball touches the ground, the scoring stops and you begin again from 0.

    Hockey Ping-pong Baseball

    Equipment Needed:

  • Bases
  • A Ping-pong ball
  • Hockey sticks

    • Object:

    Get 10 points by hitting the ball or catching the ball.


    Instructions:


  • Divide players into two teams.
  • Give out the hockey sticks to one of the teams.
  • Give the other team the bases and the ping-pong ball.
  • The team with the bases spread out on a field.
  • If they can throw and catch the ping-pong ball 5 times in a row, before the other team blocks it with a hockey stick, you get 1 point.
  • If the team with the hockey sticks can block the ball, they get a point.
  • Once a point is given, you switch positions.
  • The first team to 10 points win!

    Poison Frog

    Equipment Needed:

  • 2-4 Monkey Name Tags (1 has a green dot on the back for the Un-poisoner)
  • 2 Frog Name Tags (1 has a red dot on the back for the poison frog)

    • Object:

    Do not get “poisoned” (tagged twice) by the “poison frog.”


    Instructions:


  • Lay out the name tags and have people randomly choose their role. 1 Un-poisoner (monkey with poison vaccine), 1-3 regular monkeys, 1 Regular frog, 1 Poison frog
  • The monkeys try to eat all the frogs but not the poison frog.
  • If the monkey is tagged two times, he is poisoned and must stand still.
  • The Un-poisoner switches tags with the poisoned monkey who is now free. The original Un-poisoner is now a normal monkey and the and the original normal monkey is now the Un-poisoner.
  • You keep going till all the monkeys tagged the unpoisoner five times.
  • note: Fold the name tags in half. All the name tags should have the letter M or F (monkey or frog) on the outside. If you are a frog and a monkey tags you, flip over your card so it shows the back. If the back has red on it then it is a poison frog. If the poison frog tags you two times and you are not the unpoisoner then you need to stand still until you trade name tags with the un-poison monkey.
